When I am not working, I like to do gardening as a hobby and to swim to keep myself in shape and healthy.

Gardening

I plant all kinds of fruit trees. I keep their height small so I can manage them easily.

My spare time favorite activity is to tend the garden. I learn to prune trees to make them bear lots fruits.

I love fig trees, apple trees, avocado trees. Fig leaves are good medicine for treating many medical problems.

I also plant all kinds of flower plants. My garden is full of flowers and fruits. My friends love to enjoy and relax in my garden.

Swimming

I focus to be proficient and fast in free style swimming. I swim one hour daily in community outdoor pool in crystal clear blue water.

I push myself to swim many laps in one hour. Swimming is my only exercise.

Swimming is the best cardio exercise for everyone. You exercise every body part. Swimming has very low impact on hip joints, knee joints, and foot joints.
